What Have I Been Up To?

by Emma Gillespie


I much like anyone else have been juggling a lot of different hobbies. From reading, coding, collecting Magic The Gathering, creating a DND campaign, watching shows/movies, spending time with family, indoor golf, and many more. In doing so I have neglected this wonderful blog that I had started.

Well no more! We are back to creating YouTube videos and I will be trying to post content on here as often as I can. I think I will keep to the schedule of my Youtube posts. Once every month, on the First day of the Month I will post a video and write a blog. Maybe I will blog more often than that but at the very least there will be one post per month.

Note:

The posts may be related to the video or might not be.

What Have I Done?

There have been a few new things that I have started. One of which is a new personal project called OAISIS. I am trying to create a customizable, personified agentic AI. A partner to help organize my day, tasks, or ask questions to and get help.

I have also changed my home lab around. I now have a SIEM that will help me learn some of the Blue team side of things while being able to setup vulnerable machines and test them. This is more explained in the next section.

I have also been reading. My favourite book series of all time is Red Rising by Pierce Brown. Its as if Hungers Game, Game of Thrones, and Enders Game had a child. Follow Darrow as he causes chaos to the standard norms of society (I really do not want to spoil a whole lot). If you have not read the book I highly HIGHLY recommend doing so. There is no better book series out there.

I also have created another web application that can help me track my Magic The Gathering binders. I am trying to do a master set of all the main sets released the year of Foundations and onward to present sets coming out. This alone does take time and money. Thus, I have been working more to make up for the massive hole burning its way through my pockets. Sadly I do not have the one ring and golem is sad.

Books:

I am always looking for recomendations. Red Rising has also become super popular in recent years which is really nice to see. The first book came out in 2014 and I have been reading and following the series since 2016 when Golden Son (3rd book) was released.

What about OWASP?

I do still plan to create an OWASP Part 2. I have re-done my homelab to add an ELK stack so that I can triage or analyze the events. Allowing me to not only practice my super cool penetration hacker skills but also my analysis. I want to learn both Red and Blue team giving me the ability to go into either field. With less jobs in the area I live I may have to be a creator full time. Although my 4 views on my recent video makes that seem unlikely. Get ready because this may turn into a feet pic web store.

Jokes aside I do plan to do OWASP more. I want to be able to gain the skills and add them to a Red Team Playbook. not only becuase I will get to learn how to do the attack more in depth and hands on; but, also because I can take the ELK data and analyze it using kibana. The world of cyber sercurity is a double edged sword. To have a Blue team there must first be a Red team. To be a white hat there must first be a black hat hacker.


So all in all I do plan to do some more posts here in my blog. Making it a fun destination for ethusiast, students, and new comers to learn, read fun posts, get project ideas, or just have me ramble at them. Sadly I can't wave my hand and make you all get to this point in the post but hopefully this helped shed some light on where I have been.
